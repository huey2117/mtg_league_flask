Who are we?

In short, a random group of nerdy friends

We are an ever expanding group of nerds who play a variety of games. This particular portion of the group loves playing Magic: the Gathering, and specifically the Commander variant, so we decided to do something cool with it. We created an online EDH League played via Cockatrice utilizing themed games with budget limits and an in-house scoring system.

This site in particular came about to help us manage the league (and to help the site creator practice his python web development skills). Previously we were manually and messily managing everything inside a combination of google docs and google sheets and things like random drafts weren't able to be kept a secret, so I built this site to host various tools for us to use.

What is EDH?

Check out these links to learn more about the Commander format or some of our favorite content creators:

The Command Zone

Type: Gameplay and Meta

The Command Zone podcast is a podcast/YouTube channel hosted by Jimmy Wong and Josh Lee Kwai. Great source of info about the game of Commander and new card sets, as well as actual gameplay episodes to see in action.

The Commander's Quarters

Type: Meta and budget deck building

A YouTube channel hosted by Mitch, many episodes dedicated to building focused decks on a budget. Mitch is expanding into gameplay episodes and is one of the fastest growing Commander channels around.

Tolarian Community College

Type: Meta and Product Reviews

Hosted by The Professor, TCC provides an in-depth look at everything Magic: the Gathering and Commander. The Prof provides quality reviews of all types of Magic products (sleeves, deckboxes, etc.), always unsponsored to prevent bias.

MtG Gamepedia Wiki

Type: Wiki of all things Commander

While this isn't the "official" Wiki of Commander, it is pretty close. MTG Gamepedia has tons of great info about Magic in general as well as specific information about the Commander format and the rules differences.
