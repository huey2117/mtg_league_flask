League Rules and Scoring

Scoring Overview

  • +8/6/4/2 points for 1st, 2nd, 3rd, and 4th place respectively
  • +1 to the player who draws first blood (combat damage only)
  • +1 for casting your commander four or more times
  • +1 saving another player from elimination (max 1/opponent)
  • +1 killing anyone with commander damage (max 1 point)
  • -1 killing everyone in the same turn or going infinite
  • -1 attacking the player in sole possession of last place (in standings)
    • Only applies if 4+ players at table
    • Does not apply if they attack you first
    • Only lasts until the start of turn 5
    • If the player in last does more than 10 damage to an opponent, limitations are removed for that opponent.
  • -1 failing to cast/play your commander from the command zone
  • +1 to the player whose deck receives the most votes at the end of the game
  • +1 per challenge completed in games where challenges are included.

Rules and Restrictions

  1. Points are accrued each match, with the sum of all matches being the season score
  2. Any budget restrictions exclude commander cost and basic land cost, unless otherwise specified.
  3. Flex Cash Cards: If a budget includes flex, you receive an additional 50$ in flex cash to "buy" 3 cards. Whatever flex cash is leftover does not feed back into your deck costs.
    1. These slots exist to give players access to more expensive cards and help alleviate budget restraints and allow for the inclusion of a few 10-15$ cards in decks.
    2. Further, these slots facilitate the inclusion of fetch lands and tutors which are prohibitively expensive. Fetch lands and tutors chosen to fill flex slots cost 15$ towards your flex card budget.
  4. Budget / Deck costs are determined by Deckstats.net.
  5. A player cannot repeat a color combination between game weeks.
  6. The player in sole possession of last place goes first. In the event of a tie, a roll between the tied parties will determine who goes first.
